Plant-based products vs their meat equivalents: UK supermarket price check

Own-brand and branded pairs at the big UK chains, per 100g/100ml, following the basket in Which? (2022)
PROTOTYPE. One-day price snapshot, 6 July 2026. Daily updates and price history are planned but not live yet.
Snapshot result, 6 July 2026 Own-brand plant-based sausages still cost roughly twice as much per 100g as pork sausages at three of the four chains with a comparable pair (Tesco 1.9x, Sainsbury's 1.9x, Co-op 2.1x; Waitrose 1.1x). Burgers are near price parity (0.9x to 1.2x, with one vegetable-burger outlier at 0.4x). Own-brand vegan mayonnaise carries the largest premium in the basket (2.8x at Sainsbury's), while Heinz sells its vegan and standard mayo at the same price and size.

Method and caveats

  • Product basket: follows the own-brand and branded pairs in Which? (Dec 2022), "Plant-based alternatives can cost twice as much as meat", re-matched to July 2026 assortments. Each pair is a plant-based product and its nearest meat/egg equivalent in the same range tier.
  • Prices: scraped 6 July 2026 from trolley.co.uk (a price aggregator; secondary source) because Tesco, Asda, Ocado and Co-op block automated access to their own sites. Sainsbury's rows were verified against Sainsbury's own product API (primary source); other chains are aggregator-only in this prototype.
  • Unit prices: pounds per 100g/100ml computed from the pack price and pack weight. The aggregator's own multipack unit prices are wrong (it multiplies pack count by total weight) and were not used. Pack weights come from the retailer listing titles; the Asda veggie-burger weight is implied, not confirmed.
  • Shelf prices only: loyalty-card prices (Nectar, Clubcard) and multibuy offers are noted where seen but excluded from all figures, matching the Which? approach.
  • One day, not an average: Which? averaged three months of daily prices; this snapshot is a single day. The production version of this site will scrape daily and accumulate the same kind of average over time.
  • Coverage gaps: M&S (not on the aggregator), Morrisons sausages/burgers and Asda sausages (no comparable own-brand pair found), ready meals and coleslaw (in the Which? basket, not yet in this prototype).

What the production site adds

  • Daily automated scrape with a price-history series, so ratios become multi-month averages rather than one-day snapshots (the Which? methodology).
  • Direct retailer sources for Sainsbury's, Waitrose and Morrisons; aggregator only where retailers block access, labelled per row.
  • The remaining Which? categories (ready meals, coleslaw and potato salad) and a fuller branded set (Ginsters, plant milk).
  • Downloadable data (CSV/JSON) for every date, as on olas.kanepajs.eu.
